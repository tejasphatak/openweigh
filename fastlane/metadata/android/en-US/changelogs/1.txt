v0.1.0 — Initial release.

* Connect to BLE weight and body-composition scales and record measurements.
* Offline-first local storage of your full measurement history.
* Optional sync to Android Health Connect.
* Material 3 interface built with Jetpack Compose.
* No account, no tracking, no ads.

This is the GMS-free foss build for F-Droid: Google Drive backup and Sign in
with Google are not included.
