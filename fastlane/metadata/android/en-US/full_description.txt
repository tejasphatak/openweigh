OpenWeigh turns your Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) smart scale into a private,
offline-first health tracker. Step on the scale, and your weight and body-
composition metrics are captured straight to your phone — no account, no
sign-up, no tracking, no ads.

Everything stays on your device. Measurements are stored locally in an on-
device database, and OpenWeigh works fully offline. There are no analytics
SDKs, no telemetry, and no third-party data collection of any kind.

== Features ==

* Connect to BLE weight and body-composition scales and read measurements over
  Bluetooth.
* Track weight plus the body-composition metrics your scale reports (such as
  body fat, muscle, water, and BMI, depending on the hardware).
* Offline-first local storage — your full history lives on your device and is
  always available, with or without a network connection.
* Health Connect integration — sync your readings to Android Health Connect so
  other health and fitness apps you trust can use them, with your permission.
* Clean, modern Material 3 interface built with Jetpack Compose.
* No account required. No tracking. No ads.

== Backup ==

OpenWeigh ships in two build flavors:

* The standard flavor includes an optional Google Drive backup and Sign in with
  Google, which depend on Google Play Services (GMS).
* The F-Droid build is the GMS-free "foss" flavor. To keep it free of non-free
  dependencies, the foss build does NOT include Google Drive backup or Sign in
  with Google — those features are simply absent. Health Connect sync and full
  offline local storage work exactly the same in both flavors.

If you need cloud backup, you can still export and move your local data; the
GMS-only Drive sync is the only feature missing from the F-Droid build.

== Privacy ==

OpenWeigh is built privacy-first. Your measurements never leave your phone
unless you explicitly choose to share them with Health Connect. There are no
hidden network calls, no accounts, and no tracking.
